Christopher Luxon denies there is any problem with our tax system

Christopher Luxon has been forced to defend his party's tax policy after analysis found for every $1 in cuts the bottom half of earners get, the top 5 percent gets $10. The research, which was carried out by the Council of Trade Unions (CTU), found National's tax policies heavily benefit the wealthiest Kiwis.
